# Liyali Gateway - User Guide

## Welcome to Liyali Gateway

Liyali Gateway is a comprehensive workflow management system designed to streamline approvals, budget management, and document workflows. This guide will help you navigate the platform and accomplish your daily tasks.

---

## Getting Started

### Logging In

1. Visit the Liyali Gateway login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Enter your password
4. Click "Sign In"

**Note**: Contact your administrator if you don't have login credentials.

### First Time Setup

After your first login:

1. **Update Your Profile**
   - Click your avatar in the top-right corner
   - Go to Settings
   - Complete your profile information

2. **Set Your Preferences**
   - In Settings, choose your language, theme, and timezone
   - Enable notification preferences

3. **Review Pending Tasks**
   - Navigate to Tasks to see awaiting approvals

## Core Features

## 1. Tasks Management

### Viewing Your Tasks

1. Click **Tasks** in the sidebar
2. You'll see all pending approvals assigned to you

### Task Information

Each task shows:
- **Task Title**: What needs to be done
- **Document**: Related document number
- **Type**: Budget approval, requisition, etc.
- **Priority**: URGENT, HIGH, MEDIUM, LOW
- **Status**: PENDING, IN_PROGRESS, COMPLETED
- **Due Date**: When action is needed (highlighted if overdue)

### Task Actions

#### View Details
1. Click the **View** button on any task
2. Review the document details

#### Take Action
1. Click the **Act** button (appears for pending tasks)
2. You'll be taken to the approval screen
3. Complete the required action

### Task Filtering

Use the filter buttons at the top:
- **All**: Show all tasks
- **Pending**: Tasks awaiting your action
- **In Progress**: Tasks you've started

### Task Statistics

The top cards show your task summary:
- **Pending**: Tasks awaiting your action
- **In Progress**: Tasks you've started
- **Overdue**: Overdue tasks (shown in red)
- **Completed**: Completed tasks

## 2. Budget Management

### Viewing Budgets

1. Click **Budgets** in the sidebar under "Budget Management"
2. See all budgets organized by status and department

### Creating a New Budget

1. Click **Create Budget** button
2. Fill in the budget form:
   - **Budget Name**: Descriptive name
   - **Description**: Optional notes
   - **Department**: Select your department
   - **Fiscal Year**: Budget year (e.g., 2024)
   - **Total Amount**: Total budget allocation
   - **Currency**: ZMW or USD
3. Click **Create Budget**

### Managing Budget Items

Once your budget is created:

1. Click the budget to open details
2. Go to the **Items** tab
3. Click **Add Item** button
4. Add item details:
   - **Category**: Type of expense
   - **Description**: Item details
   - **Allocated Amount**: Money allocated
5. Items appear in the table with tracking

### Budget Tabs

#### Overview Tab
- **Total Budget**: Total allocated amount
- **Spent Amount**: Amount used (with progress bar)
- **Remaining Amount**: Available budget
- **Submit Button**: Submit for approval

#### Information Tab
- Department information
- Fiscal year
- Creator details
- Budget description

#### Items Tab
- List of all budget line items
- Category, description, allocated/spent/remaining amounts
- Add/edit items (when in DRAFT status)

#### Approvals Tab
- Approval workflow history
- Who approved and when
- Comments and remarks from approvers
- Digital signatures (thumbnail images)

### Submitting Budget for Approval

1. Ensure budget is in DRAFT status
2. Click **Submit for Approval** button
3. Budget moves to IN_APPROVAL status
4. Assigned approvers will receive notifications

## 3. Approval Workflows

### Understanding Approvals

Documents flow through approval stages based on their type and amount:

1. **DRAFT**: Initial creation
2. **SUBMITTED**: Waiting for first approver
3. **IN_APPROVAL**: Active in approval workflow
4. **APPROVED**: All stages completed
5. **REJECTED**: Returned to creator

### Approving a Document

When you need to approve something:

1. From your **Tasks** page, find the item
2. Click **Act** button
3. On approval screen:
   - Read all document details carefully
   - Add optional comments
   - **Draw your digital signature** (required)
   - Click **Confirm Approval**

#### Signature Process

1. A signature canvas appears on the screen
2. Sign using your mouse/trackpad
3. Use **Clear** to erase and try again
4. Click **Save Signature** when satisfied
5. Approval cannot proceed without a signature

### Rejecting a Document

If you need to reject:

1. From the approval screen, click **Reject** button
2. **Remarks are required** - explain why
3. Add optional additional comments if desired
4. Click **Confirm Rejection**
5. Document returns to creator with your remarks

**Important**: Detailed remarks help the creator fix issues quickly.

### Viewing Approval History

1. Open the document you're approving
2. Go to **Approvals** tab (Budgets) or **Approval History** panel
3. See each approval stage with:
   - Approver name and role
   - Timestamp of action
   - Comments provided
   - Digital signature (image)
   - Remarks if rejected

## 4. Settings & Profile

### Account Settings

To update your profile:

1. Click the **Settings** icon in the sidebar
2. Go to **Account** tab
3. Edit:
   - Name
   - Email
   - Department
   - (Role is set by administrators)
4. Click **Save Changes**

### Changing Your Password

1. Go to **Settings** → **Password** tab
2. Enter your current password
3. Enter your new password (minimum 8 characters)
4. Confirm the new password
5. Click **Update Password**

**Note**: You'll be logged out after changing your password.

In **Settings** → **General** tab, customize:

- **Language**: English, Spanish, French, Portuguese
- **Theme**: Light, Dark, or System default
- **Timezone**: Your local timezone

**Notifications**
- **Email Notifications**: For important updates
- **Push Notifications**: Browser notifications
- **Activity Notifications**: Workflow activity updates

### Managing Sessions

To view and manage your login sessions:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Sessions** tab
2. See all devices where you're logged in
3. Click **Revoke** to logout from a specific device

## Troubleshooting

### I Don't See Tasks Assigned to Me

**Solution:**
1. Refresh the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
2. Check your Timezone settings (may affect timestamps)
3. Verify your role is set correctly (contact admin)
4. Check if tasks are filtered by status

### My Signature Won't Save

**Solution:**
1. Make sure you've drawn on the canvas
2. Try clearing and redrawing
3. Ensure your browser allows canvas (check privacy settings)
4. Try a different browser

### I Can't Approve a Document

**Possible reasons:**
- Document is not in approval stage
- You're not the assigned approver
- You lack necessary permissions
- **Contact your administrator**

### Approval Takes Too Long

**Check:**
1. Are you missing required information?
2. Is your internet connection stable?
3. Try refreshing the page
4. Clear your browser cache

## Security Tips

### Protecting Your Account

1. **Strong Password**
   - Use mix of letters, numbers, symbols
   - Change password every 90 days

2. **Browser Security**
   - Log out when finished
   - Don't check "Remember me" on shared computers
   - Clear browser cache regularly

3. **Session Management**
   - Review active sessions in Settings
   - Revoke unknown sessions immediately

4. **Document Handling**
   - Don't share document links in unsecured channels
   - Use secure email for sensitive documents

## FAQ

**Q: How long do signatures stay valid?**
A: Digital signatures are valid indefinitely as part of the document record. They are stored with full audit trail.

**Q: Can I undo an approval?**
A: No, once approved, the action is permanent. Contact an administrator if correction is needed.

**Q: What if I forget my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on login page to reset.

**Q: How often should I change my password?**
A: Every 90 days is recommended for security.

**Q: Can I approve from my phone?**
A: Yes, the platform is mobile-responsive. Use a modern browser.

**Q: How long are documents kept?**
A: Per your organization's data retention policy (typically 5-7 years).
